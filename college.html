college information

Sahakar Maharshi Shankarrao Mohite Patil Institute of Technology & Research The SMSMPITR has been opened to provide engineering education to rural students. The institute runs under Shankarrao Mohite-Patil Charitable Hospital Trust. The Institute is situated in the heart of the city and it is just 1 km away from local ST bus depot. The Institute is sister concern of Sahakar Maharshi Shankarrao Mohite-Patil Sahkari Sakhar Karkhana ltd. Akluj, which is one of the largest sugar factories of Maharashtra state.
